About Me

From IT Foundations
to Data-Driven Impact

I'm Amin Alshehri, an IT graduate who discovered a passion for extracting meaning from data. My journey led me to specialize in Data Analysis—a discipline at the intersection of technology, logic, and storytelling.

Through my training at the Tabuk Health Cluster, I applied data analysis to real-world healthcare challenges—cleaning messy datasets, building dashboards that surface operational insights, and helping decision-makers act on evidence.

My IT foundation gives me a unique edge: I write clean analytical code in Python and SQL, and communicate complex findings through Power BI and Tableau visualizations.

I believe the best data analyst is not just technically proficient—they're a translator between data and strategy.
